Iam currently planning to pursue a Master's in Business Administration (MBA) after completing my bachelor's degree. Although I haven't decided on a specific institution yet, Mizzou is one of the options I am considering.

I believe that earning an MBA will provide me with advanced knowledge and skills in various areas of business, such as finance, accounting, marketing, and management. This will not only qualify me for more advanced positions in various industries, but also equip me to start my own business. I understand that many employers highly value candidates with an MBA, as it shows a strong commitment to professional development and a desire to succeed in the business world.

I am looking forward to the opportunities that an MBA program can offer, such as networking with other professionals, participating in real-world business projects, and gaining exposure to different industries and business models. I believe that these experiences will help me build a diverse skill set and a well-rounded perspective on business, which will be beneficial for my future career and personal growth.
