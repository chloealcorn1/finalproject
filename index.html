About me

Hi, my name is Chloe Alcorn and I'm a senior at the University of Missouri. Originally from Illinois, I am majoring in Economics and minoring in Political Science. Outside of my academic pursuits, I am involved in a sorority and Undergraduate Women in Economics. I'm set to graduate in August and plan to pursue a Masters in Business Administration, although I'm still deciding on where to attend. In my free time, I have a few hobbies that I enjoy. I'm an avid reader and can often be found with my nose buried in a book. I also enjoy staying active and working out, as well as spending time with friends. My family is important to me, and I am the youngest of three children. I have three young nephews and four dogs, who keep me busy and entertained. Overall, I'm excited to see what the future holds and am looking forward to the next chapter of my life.
