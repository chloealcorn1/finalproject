College Life

Home / College Life

As a senior at the University of Missouri, Chloe Alcorn has immersed herself in a variety of activities that have helped to shape her college experience. Chloe is an active member of her sorority, which has provided her with a sense of community and support throughout her college years. Being part of a sorority has given Chloe the opportunity to participate in a wide range of events, from philanthropic activities to social events, and has allowed her to form lasting friendships with other members. In addition to her involvement in her sorority, Chloe is also a member of Undergraduate Women in Economics. This organization has provided her with a platform to explore her interest in economics and connect with other women who share her passion for this field. Through this organization, Chloe has had the opportunity to attend conferences, participate in workshops, and engage in networking activities.

Chloe's major in Economics and minor in Political Science have also been central to her college experience. These fields of study have challenged her to think critically about the world around her and have equipped her with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in her future career. Chloe is set to graduate with her degree in August, and she is excited to see where her degree will take her.
